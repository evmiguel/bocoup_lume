November 22nd, 2022

I am excited to announce a new Bocoup retail training today “HTML5 For Programmers“, based on a training I’ve delivered to hundreds of web developers at some big companies over the last 6 months.

The training will run at Bocoup for 3 Days from June 1st – 3rd. I designed this training to include both in depth lectures, and hands on lab work. You can read more about the training, and sign up for a seat while they’re still hot.

Here is a sneak peak of the agenda:

Day 1

  • HTML5 Overview
  • Using HTML5 Today
  • Markup
  • Forms

Day 2

  • Canvas
  • Video and Audio
  • Drag and Drop
  • Geolocation

Day 3

  • Using HTML5 on a Mobile Device
  • Web Storage
  • Web Workers
  • Offline Web Applications
  • Communication and Web Sockets
