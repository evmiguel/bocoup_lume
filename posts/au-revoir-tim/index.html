Bye for now, Tim Branyen

news
November 22nd, 2022

The Bocoup team includes some of the smartest, most talented people we know – which has enabled us to do mission-driven work with innovative companies and developers from small startups to Mozilla, Google, and Microsoft.

Today, we share our last day with Tim Branyen and celebrate his contributions to Bocoup’s mission and culture. While at Bocoup, Tim authored his two most popular projects – Backbone Boilerplate and Layout Manager – which are in production in countless applications, and are an integral part of our Backbone workflow.

We’re excited to continue to support these libraries, and to continue a relationship with Tim as a partner in moving the Open Web forward. Great things are sure to follow in his wake!
