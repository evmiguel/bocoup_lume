Welcome Pete Karl

news
November 22nd, 2022

We are excited to welcome Pete Karl to the Bocoup team. Pete is a great programmer, and brings with him to Bocoup strong experience in systems architecture and a general hackerism that we value here.

Pete’s awesome work at Lion Burger shows an enthusiasm and talent for developing and launching great products, and a strong commitment to open source. Pete’s experience developing software for IBM, T-Mobile and Harvard Pilgrim will broaden Bocoup’s perspective.

So, follow Pete on twitter at @steyblind, and come ask him about cooking the perfect steak at the next bocoup loft event.
