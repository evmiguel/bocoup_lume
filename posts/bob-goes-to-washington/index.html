Bocoup goes to Washington

training
November 22nd, 2022

Good news, everyone – Bocoup is bringing training classes to Washington DC!

We’re pleased to announce two new trainings in the Washington, DC area with the one-and-only Richard Worth. Richard will be delivering his popular HTML5 for Programmers course April 18-20 and Comprehensive jQuery May 9-11 at the Dulles, Virginia AOL Campus.

Both courses are intended for developers who already know how to program but are ready to advance their JavaScript or jQuery skills to create more powerful websites and applications.

We’re also eager to talk to people about supporting the open web community in Northern Virginia. If you’re interested in collaborating with us on this, or just want to chat, email us.
