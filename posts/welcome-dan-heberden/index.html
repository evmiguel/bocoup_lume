Welcome Dan Heberden!

news
November 22nd, 2022

We are happy to announce that Dan Heberden will be joining the Bocoup team to work on JavaScript development, training, and contributing to open software.

Dan comes to Bocoup from his own consulting business to strengthen our powerhouse team of JavaScript programmers. Most recently, Dan’s spoken at the Bay Area jQuery Conference, and Amazon’s Developer Days Conference. He is also an active jQuery team member, and contributes bugfixes, support, and infrastructural oversight that keeps the project moving.

We are excited to have Dan on the team, and look forward to creating great things with him. Follow Dan on twitter at @danheberden, and stop by Bocoup to ask him about his JavaScript/jQuery book, which he’s writing now.
