Training: Bocoup goes to Portland

training
November 22nd, 2022

In case you haven’t heard, Bocoup has put together not one, but TWO full days of exciting JavaScript workshops in Portland, OR this May!

On May 1, the day before JSConf, there will be four workshop tracks offered. And on May 4, the day before NodeConf (and after JSConf), there will be one workshop track offered.

Each workshop track is broken up into a separate morning and afternoon session, which you can sign up for individually, or if you’re feeling adventurous, mix-and-match!

Also, these workshops will be taught by well respected, established, JavaScript experts, so you’re guaranteed to walk away from the day with a much greater understanding of things!

Here’s a list of our JavaScript experts:

  • Ben Alman
  • Paolo Fragomeni
  • Brian LeRoux
  • Max Ogden
  • Charlie Robbins
  • Luke Smith
  • Adam Sontag
  • Marak Squires

So, what are you waiting for? If you, or anyone else you know, is interested in learning a whole lot more about JavaScript, sign up now.
