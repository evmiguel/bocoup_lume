Welcome Greg Smith

news
November 22nd, 2022

We are excited to welcome Greg Smith to the Bocoup team. Greg brings with him a wealth of experience in web development and software R&D which he is now bringing to the open web.

He’re currently developing a Kickstarter-funded large-scale HTML5 RPG, and likes to build & release small tools like http://jsregex.com. He’s interested in games, game development tools, and is looking for open source projects that want their UI spruced-up. He’ll also be working with Darius Kazemi on a pre-conference training for Bocoup’s upcoming New Game Conference.

Follow Greg on twitter at @_gsmith, or stop by Bocoup to ask him about his passion for electronic music.
