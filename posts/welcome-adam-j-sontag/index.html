Welcome Adam J. Sontag

news
November 22nd, 2022

We are very excited to welcome Adam J. Sontag to Bocoup. Adam is a member of the jQuery UI team, and a co-host of the yayQuery Podcast. Adam is also a prolific community support leader in the jQuery forums and in #jquery on freenode.

Adam joins us from his family’s financial software business, and brings a great deal of complex client side JavaScript experience to Bocoup.

Adam will be finishing up some current projects, and joining the Bocoup team in January. Be sure to follow Adam on twitter at @ajpiano, and feel free to stop by and ask him about making the chocolate with the widget factory.
