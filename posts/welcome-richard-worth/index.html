Welcome Richard Worth

news
November 22nd, 2022

We are pleased to welcome Richard Worth to Bocoup. Richard brings 12 years of network software architecture and development experience to the team as well as a knack and passion for working with and evangelizing new HTML5 APIs.

Richard is a member of the jQuery team and project lead for jQuery UI, where he has been leading development efforts on the jQuery UI grid, and jQuery UI 1.9.

Richard will continue to work on jQuery UI and HTML5 training and evangelism at Bocoup. We are very excited to have Richard join us, and look forward to supporting his leadership in the success of jQuery UI and HTML5! Stop by Bocoup to ask Richard about using Dojo to structure your large scale web applications.
