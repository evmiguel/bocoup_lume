Welcome Rebecca Murphey

news
November 22nd, 2022

We are excited to announce that Rebecca Murphey will be joining Bocoup’s consulting team in May. Rebecca’s commitment to the Open Web is very aligned with Bocoup’s mission, and she’s been a great collaborator over the years.

Rebecca’s roles as a host on YayQuery, creator of TXJS, and author of the jQuery Fundamentals book are just a few of her accomplishments as a leader in the Open Web and JavaScript communities.

Rebecca has also acted as a thought leader in the JavaScript community around large-scale JavaScript application development and code organization, as well as driving reflective discussions about the future of JavaScript community.

We are honored to have Rebecca join our team, and look forward to moving the Open Web forward together!
