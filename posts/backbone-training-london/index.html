Announcing Backbone.js Training in London

training web applications
November 22nd, 2022

Bocoup has put down roots in communities across the globe. We’ve traveled to Turkey, Brazil, England, France, Canada, Germany and the Netherlands to work with developers the world over. It’s exciting to see that our mission to move the Open Web forward is shared internationally, and we’re eager to continue working with these communities through events and workshops.

Today, we’re excited to announce that we’re heading back to London to deliver our popular Backbone.js training with Irene Ros on October 17. This three-day immersive Backbone workshop takes developers from intro to completion of a Backbone application. We’ll cover testing and debugging Backbone, Backbone routes, history management and real-time applications in addition to models, collections and views. It will be an intimate and engaging workshop, so space is limited. Reserve your tickets here.
