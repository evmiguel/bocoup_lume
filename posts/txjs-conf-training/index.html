Announcing Sponsorship and Training at TXJS

training
November 22nd, 2022

We’re excited to announce our sponsorship of TXJS 2012!

TXJS’s community-driven approach and commitment to quality content aligns perfectly with Bocoup’s mission to move the Open Web forward through education, community development, and consulting. We’re proud to partner with TXJS to promote these initiatives and give back to our community.

Bocoup will also host a pre-conference JavaScript workshop on June 13 with Ben Alman. Topics to be covered include Immediately Invoked Function Expressions, the module pattern, type coercion, and more. Workshop tickets also include tickets to the pre- and post-conference parties. Sign up here.

For questions about the course, email us or connect with us on Twitter at @bocouptraining.
